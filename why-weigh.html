Why Weigh Your RV

Most RVs are not loaded evenly. Even if your total weight is under limits, individual axles or wheels may be overloaded.

  • Improves tire safety and longevity
  • Helps prevent suspension and axle damage
  • Improves handling and braking
  • Provides peace of mind while traveling

Knowing your real weights allows you to travel safer and smarter.

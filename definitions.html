RV Definitions

GVWR (Gross Vehicle Weight Rating)

The maximum allowable weight of the fully loaded RV, including cargo, fluids, passengers, and accessories.

GAWR (Gross Axle Weight Rating)

The maximum weight that a single axle is designed to safely support.

Payload Capacity

The amount of weight you can safely add to your RV, including gear, water, and passengers.

Tongue Weight

The downward force exerted on the hitch by the trailer, critical for towing safety and stability.

Four-Corner Weighing

A weighing method that measures the weight at each wheel position to identify imbalances and safety concerns.
